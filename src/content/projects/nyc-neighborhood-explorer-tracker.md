  - date: 'Jul 23 2026'
    note: 'I started to build an MVP consisting of _just_ the tracking feature. I was using the official government''s geo json. However, it doesn''t not truly reflect the neighborhoods. For example, it lumps carroll gardens with gowanus and red hook. Luckily I found a more accurate representation. A new problem arose for me. Neighborhood lines changes frequently and every one''s perception is different. For example, Hamilton Heights is part of Harlem, and the boundary between Prospect Lefferts Gardens and Crown Heights is blurring (in part of real estate agents trying to market the price higher). This I am still thinking, if it even matters at all.'
